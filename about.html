About Me

Biography

I obtained my PhD in Computer Science at Virginia Tech in 2020.

I am broadly interested in computing education and software engineering. I work on methods to help students improve elements of their personal software process like incremental development, test writing, and test quality. A primary focus has been to help student software developers acquire procedural knowledge about software development by providing them with formative feedback about their development habits as they work on projects.

Find out more about my current and past work here.

I'm originally from Mumbai, India and have been living in the United States since 2011.
