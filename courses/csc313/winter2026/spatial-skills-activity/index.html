CSC 313 Spatial skills activity

To aid in today’s discussion, we’re going to do some spatial skills activities. These should help us get a stronger sense for what exactly we mean by “spatial skills”. Our goal here is not so much to get the right answers, but rather to “think about our thinking” when solving these problems.

The following questions are adapted from a free worksheet from happyneuronpro.com.

Task: Complete at least 5 of these questions. In the text entry box, write your answers and also your articulation for the process you employed to answer the question.
