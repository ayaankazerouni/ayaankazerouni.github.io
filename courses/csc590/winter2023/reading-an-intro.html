Reading an introduction

Read the introductions of the following papers:

  • Breaking Through Binaries: Compiler-quality Instrumentation for Better Binary-only Fuzzing (Nagy et al.)
  • Exploiting Input Sanitization for Regex Denial of Service (Barlas, Du, & Davis)

For each paper, write a bulleted list of short statements. The list should contain:

  • Context
  • Prior work in the area
  • Gaps in prior work
  • What the paper does to address the gaps
  • Results, if applicable

You don’t have to write exactly 5 bullet points—any of the items above may need multiple bullet points.
